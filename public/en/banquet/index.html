Group Packages

We cater for group events from 10 to 80 people. In conjuction with our partner Cable Car Adliswil-Felsenegg LAF, we offer a selection of all inclusive packages. We also offer tailor made group reservations from our group menu. All menus can be adjusted according to vegitarian or allergy requirements.

For reservations please contact Sihltal Zürich Uetliberg Bahn SZU AG via email at szuextra@szu.ch or phone 044 206 45 07
